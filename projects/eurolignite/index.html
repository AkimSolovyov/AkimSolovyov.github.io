Introduction

Eurolignit Company – is a trading house that is engaged in realization of lignite and production of organic bio-fertilizer. We are young upcoming company; our main activity is the sale of coal products and production of bio-fertilizers based on humic acids from lignite of our own mining.

Eurolignit Company created as a separate unit of the mining enterprise "Energy Investment Company" for the trading and distribution of coal products and related materials. Our parent company has one of the largest in Ukraine deposits of lignite, and for a long time is a successful supplier of products in the domestic Ukrainian market and the markets of neighboring countries.

  • Evolution

    Establishment of the Trade House is associated with the desire to develop new directions for the implementation of a wide range of coal products, as well as the organization of production of humic acid from lignite, which according to its physicochemical properties is a material optimally suitable for producing high-quality biological fertilizers.

    Currently the company plans to conduct wide scale research and agronomic approbation and bring to market an entirely new, unique line of humic fertilizer using a patented know-how under its own brand.

  • New Horizons

    Elaborating production cycle of the enterprise built according to the best international standards, to match the highest quality standards. The company plans to purchase a modern high-tech equipment that will produce more than 1,000 tons of humic concentrate in a year. We are recruiting highly qualified scientific and technical staff, who will ensure the best results in the production of the entire range of products.

    The company works on development of its own distribution network and actively cooperates with other producers of fertilizers and agrochemicals.
